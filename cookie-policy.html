This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how DigitalServicesHub ("we", "us", or "our") uses them on our website (https://digitalserviceshub.online), and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g., secure login, page navigation).
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., page views, time spent).
  • Advertising Cookies: To display relevant advertisements to you via Google AdSense.

3. Cookies We Use

A. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features. Without these cookies, services like secure login areas cannot be provided.

B. Performance & Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our website. usage data is anonymized and helps us improve the site. These cookies track metrics such as:

  • The number of visitors to our site.
  • Which pages are visited most frequently.
  • Error messages provided to users.

C. Advertising & Targeting (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • DoubleClick Cookie: Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt-out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie rights by setting your browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.

To learn more about how to manage cookies, visit All About Cookies.
