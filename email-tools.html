Mastering the Email Generator

Follow this comprehensive guide to go from a simple idea to a high-converting campaign in under 3 minutes.

01

Select Your Tool

Choose the right tool based on your specific goal. Use the Subject Line Generator for boosting initial opens, or the Sequence Planner if you're building a 5-day welcome journey.

Each tool utilizes a specific AI model optimized for that format—newsletters focus on engagement, while cold emails focus on concise direct response.

02

Input Your Context

The secret to great AI copy is context. Instead of typing "Sale," try "50% off eco-friendly yoga mats for Earth Day."

Specify your audience, the main benefit, and any deadline. The more specific you are, the more the AI can personalize the persuasion triggers to your specific niche.

03

Refine & Deploy

Review the generated outputs. Our system often provides 3-5 variations. Mix and match parts of each to create your "Ultimate Version."

Click Copy and paste directly into your favorite email provider (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, Apollo, etc.). We recommend A/B testing two of the generated subject lines for best results.

Yes. Because our AI generates responses based on your specific input in real-time, the results are unique. Unlike "templates" where everyone sends the same text, our AI creates custom copy for your specific offer every time you click generate.
